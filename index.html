A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Southport, North Carolina, with my three beautiful daughters. My journey into tech started at a very young age. From the very first time I got to play (a game) on my moms old IBM PC I was spellbound, and since then the continuing advancements in tech has held my interest over the years. It has always been something that I have wanted to be a part of in any and every way posible. It's amazing to me how quickly things went from DOS games to such a large marjority of people/businesses having their own personal website('s). How it has become almost a necesity to make your presence known on the web to get your name, your company, your ideas/designs/creations YOUR VOICE out there for the entire world to see and hear.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs. It amazed me how easy it became to make your presence known on the web and how with alittle hard work, some great design ideas, and creativity any individual could get their voice and ideas out there for the entire world to see and hear or at very least the select communities or groups of people that one may choose. I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
